Admission Process

Simple and transparent steps to join our school

1

Registration

Fill out the online registration form with basic information about your child and family.

2

Document Submission

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and address proof.

3

Entrance Test

Appear for an entrance assessment to evaluate academic level and aptitude.

4

Interview

Attend a personal interview with our admission team to discuss your child's goals.

5

Admission Letter

Receive admission confirmation and enrollment details via email or post.

6

Enrollment

Complete final enrollment formalities and welcome to our school family!

Eligibility & Requirements

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: 2.5 - 3.5 years
  • KG: 3.5 - 4.5 years
  • Grade 1: 5+ years
  • Other grades: As per CBSE guidelines

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy)
  • Previous School Records
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill/Ration Card)
  • Passport Size Photographs (4 copies)

Important Information

  • Admission is merit-based and competitive
  • Reservation as per government norms
  • Admission fee is non-refundable
  • Decisions are final and binding

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the admission process start?

The admission process typically starts in December and continues until all seats are filled. Early registration is recommended.

Is there a scholarship program?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial assistance for deserving students. Contact our office for details.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio of 1:25 to ensure personalized attention and quality education.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services. Additional charges apply for this facility.

Can I visit the school before admission?

Absolutely! We encourage campus visits. Please contact our office to schedule a tour at your convenience.
